Semiconductor Industry

Median Salary: $170k

Silicon Photonics & Optical Interconnect Engineer

Industry Context: Demand is strongest around optical interconnects, co-packaged optics, and photonic accelerators that support neural networks. Companies include Ayar Labs, Celestial AI, Intel, AMD, Coherent, GlobalFoundries, and Apple.

Recommended Courses

ECE 2272 Simulation and Design of Silicon Photonics
ECE 2240 Nano-Optics
ECE 3235 Semiconductor Lasers
ECE 2231 Fundamentals of Semiconductor and Quantum Electronic Devices
ECE 2192 Introduction to VLSI Design
ECE 2237 Analog Circuit Design

Semiconductor Process Development/Integration Engineer

Industry Context: CHIPS Act investments have created strong demand at Intel, TSMC (Arizona), Samsung, GlobalFoundries, and Micron. Focus on advanced node development, process integration, and yield engineering.

Recommended Courses
